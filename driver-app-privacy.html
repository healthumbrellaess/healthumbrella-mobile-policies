Privacy Policy for HU MediRoute

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Health Umbrella ("we," "our," or "us") provides the HU MediRoute mobile application ("App") for ambulance drivers and emergency medical service providers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Information

  • Continuous GPS Tracking: We collect real-time GPS location data continuously while you're on duty to enable live ambulance tracking.
  • Background Location: Location data is collected in the background even when the app is not actively in use, to ensure uninterrupted tracking during emergency response.
  • Route Data: We track routes, speed, and navigation history for service optimization and safety.
  • Google Maps Integration: Location and route data is processed through Google Maps API for navigation and mapping.

2.2 Driver and Vehicle Information

  • Driver identification and credentials
  • Vehicle information and registration details
  • Emergency response assignments
  • Duty status and availability

2.3 Device Information

  • Device type, model, and unique identifiers
  • Operating system version
  • Mobile network information and connectivity status
  • App version and performance metrics

2.4 Service and Performance Data

  • Response times and trip history
  • Service completion records
  • Emergency call logs and dispatch information
  • App usage patterns and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Real-Time Tracking: To share your location with dispatch centers and patients waiting for ambulance services
  • Route Optimization: To provide navigation and optimal routing for emergency response
  • Fleet Management: To monitor and coordinate ambulance fleet operations
  • Emergency Response: To connect you with patients and emergency service requests
  • Performance Analytics: To analyze response times, service quality, and operational efficiency
  • Safety & Compliance: To ensure driver safety and regulatory compliance
  • Communication: To send dispatch notifications, updates, and emergency alerts

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure data storage using Firebase Realtime Database
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and compliance monitoring
  • Backup and disaster recovery procedures

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps API: For real-time navigation, route planning, and location tracking (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase: For real-time database, cloud messaging, and analytics (subject to Firebase Privacy Policy)

6. Location Data Permissions

The App requires comprehensive location permissions to function:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION: For precise GPS tracking required for emergency response
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: For network-based location when GPS is unavailable
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION: For continuous tracking during duty hours, even when app is in background
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_LOCATION: To maintain tracking during active emergency responses

Important: These permissions are essential for emergency medical services. Denying these permissions will prevent the app from functioning properly.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share your location and service data as necessary for emergency services:

  • Patients and Users: Your real-time location is shared with patients and users who have requested ambulance services
  • Dispatch Centers: Location and status information is shared with emergency dispatch and fleet management systems
  • Healthcare Facilities: Trip and arrival information may be shared with receiving hospitals and medical facilities
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law enforcement or for legal compliance
  • Service Providers: With authorized third-party providers who support app operations (under confidentiality agreements)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

8. Your Rights

As an app user, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal and location data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to certain data processing activities
  • Request data portability
  • Go offline/on-duty to control location sharing

Note: Some rights may be limited due to the critical nature of emergency medical services and regulatory requirements.

9. Employment and Professional Use

This App is intended for professional use by ambulance drivers and emergency medical service personnel. Your employer or service provider may have additional policies regarding app use, data access, and monitoring during work hours.

10. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Active Location Data: Retained for the duration of active duty shifts and emergency responses
  • Historical Location Data: Retained for up to 90 days for service analysis and improvement
  • Trip and Service Records: Retained as required by healthcare regulations and legal requirements
  • Account Information: Retained for the duration of your employment/service agreement

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Important changes will be communicated through the App. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Compliance

This App complies with applicable data protection laws and healthcare regulations, including but not limited to HIPAA requirements for medical service providers where applicable.

13. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.
