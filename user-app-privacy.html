Privacy Policy for HU MediTrack

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Health Umbrella ("we," "our," or "us") provides the HU MediTrack mobile application ("App") to help users track and locate ambulances in real-time. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Information

  • Real-time Location Data: We collect your device's real-time GPS location when you use the App to track ambulances near you.
  • Background Location: With your permission, we may collect location data in the background to provide continuous ambulance tracking and emergency services.
  • Google Maps Integration: Location data is processed through Google Maps API for mapping and navigation purposes.

2.2 Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information
  • App version and usage data

2.3 User Account Information

  • Contact information (if provided)
  • Emergency contact details (optional)
  • Booking and request history

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: To provide real-time ambulance tracking and location services
  • Emergency Response: To connect you with nearby ambulances during medical emergencies
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality
  • Communication: To send service-related notifications and updates
  • Safety & Security: To ensure safe and reliable emergency medical services

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use Firebase services for secure data storage and real-time updates
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps API: For location tracking and mapping (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase: For real-time database and notifications (subject to Firebase Privacy Policy)

6. Location Data Permissions

The App requires location permissions to function properly:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION: For precise location tracking
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: For approximate location when precise location is unavailable
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION: For continuous ambulance tracking even when app is in background

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, but this may limit app functionality.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in these circumstances:

  • Emergency Services: Location data is shared with ambulance drivers and emergency responders to facilitate medical assistance
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety and rights
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in app operations (subject to confidentiality agreements)

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking
  • Object to data processing
  • Request data portability

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Location data may be retained for emergency response purposes and service improvement.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
